Welcome to The Olongapo City Mardi Gras

The Olongapo City Mardi Gras is a vibrant festival that celebrates the colorful culture and spirit of Olongapo City. This annual event originated in 1981 and has evolved into a three-day extravaganza featuring lively parades, captivating performances by acclaimed bands and artists, and a diverse array of delicious food offerings. The Mardi Gras brings together the community, allowing them to celebrate and create lasting memories. THIS IS A CELEBRATION YOU WON'T WANT TO MISS!
