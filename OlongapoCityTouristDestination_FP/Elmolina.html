Welcome to Elmolina Beach Resort

ELMOLINA BEACH RESORT

Elmolina Beach Resort in Barrio Barretto, Olongapo, Philippines offers a tropical retreat with over 20 air-conditioned rooms equipped with modern amenities and native nipa huts for a traditional experience. The resort provides a serene environment, ideal for team buildings, family gatherings, birthdays, and customized group packages. Guests can indulge in water sports activities such as Jet Skiing, kayaking, island hopping, and snorkeling or enjoy a game of beach volleyball.

Located in: Barrio Barretto Address: 135 RH 5 National Hwy, Olongapo, 2200 Zambales
