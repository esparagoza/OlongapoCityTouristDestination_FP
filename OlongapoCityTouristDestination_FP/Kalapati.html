Welcome to Kalapati, Olongapo City

Kalapati (Dove Monument)

Olongapo City boasts a significant landmark that serves as a powerful tribute to its war history - the World Peace Memorial. This renowned attraction draws tourists from far and wide, thanks to its 'Kalapati' or Dove monument. This stunning structure was built to honor the lives lost during the tragic battle at Zigzag Pass - a battle that is widely regarded as one of the most devastating and protracted of World War II. The memorial is located at the intersection of the national highway and the entry road to New Cabalan, ensuring it is easily accessible to all visitors.

Located in: Bataan Road, Olongapo Address: R8XG+P8W, Jose Abad Santos Ave, Olongapo, Zambales
