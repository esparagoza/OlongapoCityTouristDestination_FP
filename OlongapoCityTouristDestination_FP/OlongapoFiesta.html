Welcome to The Olongapo City Fiesta Parade

The Olongapo City Fiesta Parade is a yearly event that commemorates the feast of Our Lady of the Most Holy Rosary, who is the patron saint of the city. The parade features intricately designed floats, lively marching bands, and cultural performers that exhibit the diverse traditions and rich heritage of Olongapo City. The parade is a vibrant and colorful celebration that attracts both residents and tourists to witness its festive atmosphere and enjoy the cultural presentations.
