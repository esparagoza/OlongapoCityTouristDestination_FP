Welcome to The Sibit-Sibit Summer Festival, Olongapo City

The Sibit-Sibit Summer Festival takes place in Olongapo City every year during the 4th week of April, specifically in Brgy. Barreto. It aims to celebrate the city's history as an ancient fishing village and promote tourism in the area. The festival offers a wide range of exciting activities, such as banca races, a fluvial parade, sail show, beach volleyball, bangkang buhangin sculpting competition, bingo bonanza, and a summer dance craze. The festival's name comes from the small paddle-driven bancas used for fishing in the area during the early days when Brgy. Barreto was known as Sitio Maquinaya. The festival was first held in 1996 and was revived by the city government in 2006.
