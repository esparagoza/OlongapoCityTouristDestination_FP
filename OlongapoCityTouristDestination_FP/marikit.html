Welcome to Marikit Park, Olongapo City

MARIKIT PARK

Marikit Park is situated at the center of Olongapo City, this park is ideal for the children and young at heart who simply wants to have a relaxation. A scenic playground and benches are provided in this place. Marikit Park is the place where students love to hang out and spent their free time here. It is also the one of the earliest parks of Olongapo. It has become famous in its time.

Located in: SM City Olongapo Central Address: R7PM+X77, Hospital Rd, Olongapo, Zambales
